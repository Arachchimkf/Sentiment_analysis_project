
Sentiment Analysis Dataset - Binary Classification

About Dataset

Sentiment analysis uses natural language processing and machine learning techniques to 
analyze the emotional tone or sentiment behind a piece of text. It involves identifying 
and categorizing opinions expressed in a text as positive, negative, or neutral.
This dataset contains different kinds of tweets and their sentiment (0 and 1).
